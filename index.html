Bringing Smiles Foundation
- A Step Towards Humanity

A registered non-governmental organization (ngo) with the Government of karnataka, "Bringing Smiles Foundation" - A Step Toward Humanity - Serves the needs of students in government Schools and runs job-skills-based programs for young people, Women and people with disabilities, among others, Needs. Since 2009, bringing smiles has provided school Supplies to more than 44,150 students, set up 15 "Free Computer labs," trained women in self-employment, and helped People access the other services they need to succeed, like English language instruction, additional education, access to Childcare and transportation.
