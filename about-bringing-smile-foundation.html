About Bringing Smiles

Get To Know About Our Bringing Smiles Foundation

The Bringing Smiles Foundation (BSF), The bringing smiles foundation (bsf), established in 2009, is a national volunteer organisation that encourages young people, women, individuals with disabilities, and others to volunteer and offers them worthwhile chances to do good in the world. Bsf seeks to influence society for the better by fostering a culture of volunteerism.

Our Achivements

The Bring Smile Foundation's
Impact and Compassion

EERS

Empowering Education Transforming Lives

Through the EERA program, the bring smile foundation has worked diligently to bridge the gap in educational access for underprivileged students. By partnering with local schools and community organizations, we’ve been able to identify and support students who lack the resources to succeed academically. Our efforts have included providing textbooks, learning materials.

WISE

WISE Program’s Impact on Women’s Empowerment in Bengaluru

Through our wise (women’s initiative for social empowerment) program, we have uplifted over 8,000 women across bengaluru by providing them with essential skills training, educational workshops, and economic opportunities. This initiative has been instrumental in fostering independence and self-confidence among women.

SAVE

SAVE’s Role in Transforming Lives Post-Environmental Crises

Our save (support and aid for vulnerable environments) initiative has been a lifeline for over 15,000 individuals facing the aftermath of environmental crises. By providing immediate relief and long-term resources, save has played a crucial role in helping communities rebuild their lives and strengthen their resilience against future challenges.
