Women empowerment refers to the creation of an environment for women where they can make decisions of their own. For their personal benefits as well as for society. Women's economic empowerment refers to the ability for women to Enjoy their rights to control and benefit from resources, assets, income, and their own time, as well as the ability To manage risk and improve their economic status and well-being.

Empowering Women Through Self-Employment

At bsf charity, we believe in the transformative power of self-employment for women. Our "Women in self-employment" initiative is dedicated to providing women with the resources, training, and support they need to build their own businesses and achieve financial independence. Through mentorship programs, microloans, and entrepreneurial workshops, we help women turn their skills and passions into successful ventures. Join us in supporting these inspiring women as they create brighter futures for themselves and their communities.


    • Providing essential training and mentorship to empower women with business skills and confidence.
    • Offering microloans and resources to help women start or grow their own businesses.
